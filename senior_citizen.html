About the Document

A Senior Citizen Certificate (or Card) is an official identification document issued by Indian state governments to individuals aged 60 and above.

Eligibility

  • Age: Must be 60 years or older.
  • Citizenship: Must be a citizen of India.
  • Residency: Valid proof of permanent residency in the state where applying.

Required Documents

  • Age Proof:Birth certificate, Passport, PAN Card, or School Leaving Certificate.
  • Address Proof: Ration Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Electricity/Telephone Bill.
  • Identity Proof:Voter ID, PAN Card, or Aadhaar Card.
  • Photographs: Passport-sized photographs.
  • Medical Certificate: In some cases, blood group information may be required.

Application Process

  1. Register: Visit the Aaple Sarkar portal and create an account.
  2. Log in: Enter credentials to access the dashboard.
  3. Fill Application Form: Complete the form with personal and address details.
  4. Upload Documents: Scan and upload required documents, including photos and proof of residence.
  5. Pay Fee: Remit the application fee.
  6. Submit & Track: Submit the form and use the generated application number to track status.
Fees: ranging from free to approximately ₹50–₹800,
Processing Time: 7–30 working days

Where to Apply

Online: Aaple Sarkar Portal
Offline: Nearest Jan Seva Kendra
