About the Document

An Income Certificate is an official government document issued by the Revenue Department that certifies the annual income of an individual or family from all sources. It is commonly required to avail government schemes, scholarships, fee concessions, reservation benefits, and other welfare services.

Eligibility

  • The applicant must be a citizen of India.
  • The applicant must be a resident of the concerned state.
  • The certificate is issued based on the income of the individual or family.
  • Income details must be supported by valid documents.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill / Rent Agreement / Property Tax Receipt)
  • School Leaving Certificate / Birth Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Proof:
    • Salary Slip / Form 16 (for salaried persons)
    • Income Tax Return (if available)
    • Self-declaration for unemployed / farmers
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Self-declaration / Affidavit
  • Original documents must be produced for verification

Application Process

  • Online Process
    1. Visit Aaple Sarkar / MahaDBT portal
    2. Register or Login using mobile/Aadhaar
    3. Select Income Certificate service
    4. Fill application form
    5. Upload required documents
    6. Pay applicable fee
    7. Submit form and note Application ID
    8. Track status and download certificate after approval
  • Offline Process
    1. Visit Tehsil / Taluka Office or Setu / Maha e-Seva Kendra
    2. Collect Income Certificate application form
    3. Fill form and attach required documents
    4. Submit form and get acknowledgement receipt
    5. Verification by Revenue Officer
    6. Collect Income Certificate
Fees:
Online Application: ₹15 – ₹50
Offline (Setu / Maha e-Seva): ₹20 – ₹30 service charge

Processing Time:
Online: 7 – 15 working days
Offline: 10 – 20 working days

Where to Apply

Online: Aaple Sarkar Portal
Offline: Tehsildar Office / Setu / Maha e-Seva Kendra
