Death Certificate

Required Documents

  1. Medical Cause of Death Certificate (Form 4 / 4A)
  2. Aadhaar Card of the deceased person
  3. Aadhaar Card of the applicant
  4. Application Form (Form No. 2)
  5. Letter from Society / Police (if death occurred at home)
  6. Cremation Ground Receipt ( श्मशानभूमीची पावती )
Important Note:
If the death certificate is not issued within 21 days, then late fees will be applicable.
Late Fee: ₹20 – ₹50
