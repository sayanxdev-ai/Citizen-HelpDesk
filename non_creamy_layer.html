About the document

The "Non-Creamy Layer" (NCL) refers to the economically less advanced segment within India's Other Backward Classes (OBC) category, identified by a government certificate for those whose family income falls below a certain threshold (currently ₹8 Lakhs/year), enabling access to affirmative action benefits like job reservations, fee waivers, and scholarships for education and government schemes to promote socio-economic upliftment

Eligibility

  1. Income Limit: Total family income below ₹8,00,000 per annum (salary and agricultural income often excluded from calculation).
  2. Socio-Economic Status: Based on family income, asset ownership, and parental employment in government/international organizations.

Required Documents

  1. School Leaving Certificate
  2. Birth Certificate
  3. Benefeciary Caste Certificate
  4. Aadhar Card
  5. Ration Card
  6. 3 Years Income Certificate
  7. Light Bill or Property Tax Receipt
  8. Affidavit
