About Ration Card

A Ration Card is an official government document issued by the State Government that enables households to purchase essential commodities at subsidized rates. It also serves as an important identity and address proof.

Eligibility
  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Applicant should be a resident of the state
  • Applicant must not possess another ration card
Required Documents
  • Aadhaar Card of all family members
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill / Rent Agreement)
  • Income Certificate
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Cancellation Certificate (if old ration card exists)
Application Process
  • Fill the ration card application form
  • Submit required documents
  • Verification by Food & Civil Supplies Department
  • Approval of application
  • Receive Ration Card
Fees
₹20 – ₹50
Processing Time
10 – 30 working days
Where to Apply
  • Online:State Food & Civil Supplies Portal
  • Offline: Ration Office / Seva Kendra / CSC
