About the Document

A caste certificate is an official Indian government document verifying a person's membership in a specific caste, such as Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), or Other Backward Classes (OBC), serving as crucial proof for accessing government benefits like educational reservations, job quotas, scholarships, and welfare schemes.

Eligibility

  • The applicant must be a citizen of India.
  • The individual must belong to a community that is officially recognized and listed as a Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or Other Backward Class (OBC) under the relevant Presidential Orders of the Indian Constitution (Articles 341 and 342).
  • The applicant must be a permanent resident of the specific state or Union Territory from which they are applying for the certificate.
  • The applicant should not already hold a duplicate caste certificate.

Required Documents

  • Documents Required for OBC / SBC Caste Certificate
    •
    • School Leaving Certificate of the applicant / Bonafide Certificate / Birth Certificate
    • Valid documentary proof showing that any one blood relative of the applicant was residing in Maharashtra before 1967
    • Caste Certificate of any one blood relative of the applicant
    • Ration Card or valid proof of residence
    • Election (Voter ID) Card
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Self-declaration / Affidavit
    • Original documents must be produced for verification
  • Documents Required for SC / ST Caste Certificate
    •
    • School Leaving Certificate of the applicant / Bonafide Certificate / Birth Certificate
    • Valid documentary proof showing that any one blood relative of the applicant was residing in Maharashtra before 1950
    • Caste Certificate of any one blood relative of the applicant
    • Ration Card or valid proof of residence
    • Election (Voter ID) Card
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Self-declaration / Affidavit
    • Original documents must be produced for verification
  • Documents Required for VJNT Caste Certificate
    •
    • School Leaving Certificate of the applicant / Bonafide Certificate / Birth Certificate
    • Valid documentary proof showing that any one blood relative of the applicant was residing in Maharashtra before 1961
    • Caste Certificate of any one blood relative of the applicant
    • Ration Card or valid proof of residence
    • Election (Voter ID) Card
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Self-declaration / Affidavit
    • Original documents must be produced for verification

Application Process

  • Online Process
    1. Visit Aaple Sarkar / MahaDBT portal
    2. Register or Login using mobile/Aadhaar
    3. Select Caste Certificate service
    4. Fill application form
    5. Upload required documents
    6. Pay fee (if applicable)
    7. Submit form and note Application ID
    8. Track status online and download certificate after approval
  • Offline Process
    1. Visit Tehsil / Taluka Office or Setu / Maha e-Seva Kendra
    2. Collect application form
    3. Fill form and attach documents
    4. Submit form and get acknowledgement
    5. Verification by revenue officer
    6. Collect caste certificate
Fees:
General Category: ₹20 – ₹50
SC / ST / OBC / VJNT: Usually Free
Setu / Maha e-Seva Center: ₹20 – ₹30 service charge (if applied offline)

Processing Time:
Online Application: 7 – 21 working days
Offline Application: 15 – 30 working days

Where to Apply

Online: Aaple Sarkar Portal
Offline: Tehsildar Office/ Setu / Maha e-Seva Kendra
