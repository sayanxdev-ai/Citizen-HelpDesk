Passport

About the Document

A Passport is an official travel document issued by the Government of India that certifies the identity and nationality of an individual for international travel.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Minimum age as per government rules

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Address Proof
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport-size Photographs

Application Process

  1. Fill the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fees
  4. Visit Passport Office for verification
  5. Receive Passport
Fees: ₹1500 (Normal) | ₹3500 (Tatkal)
Processing Time: 15–30 working days

Where to Apply

Online: Official Government Portal
Offline: Nearest Passport Seva Kendra
